General Rules

  • You will be directed to the next question when you type the correct answer to the current question.
  • Each question has the same weightage and if two people have the same number of questions answered then time will be used as the differentiating factor.

Rules related to answering the questions

  • One person can play on only one account. With no negative marking whatsoever, there is no reason to make multiple accounts as it is.
  • CAPS and SPACES are not allowed. Eg: If your answer is "Shreyas Bapat", enter "shreyasbapat" to proceed to the next level. The answers also do not have special characters such as !, " etc. but 0,1,2,....,9 are exceptions.
  • Beware of spelling errors in your answers. Game can't auto correct your spellings!
